Independent Sales Representative Agreement
Commission-Only · United Kingdom

This agreement is made between:

Company: CrftdWeb, operated by Obinna Eze-Elijah ("the Company")
Email: admin@crftdweb.com · Website: crftdweb.com

Representative: The individual who accepts this agreement via the CrftdWeb offer system ("the Representative")

The Representative's name, email, and date of acceptance are recorded digitally at the time of acceptance.

1. Appointment

The Company appoints the Representative as a non-exclusive, independent sales representative to introduce potential clients to the Company's web design and development services. This agreement does not constitute employment, a partnership, or a joint venture.

2. Independent Contractor Status

The Representative is an independent contractor, not an employee of the Company. The Representative:

  1. Is responsible for their own tax, National Insurance, and any other statutory obligations arising from income earned under this agreement.
  2. Has no entitlement to employee benefits including holiday pay, sick pay, pension contributions, or redundancy pay.
  3. May work for other businesses or clients, provided there is no direct conflict of interest with the Company.
  4. Is free to set their own hours and working methods.

3. Commission

  1. Rate: The Representative will earn a tiered commission for each client they introduce who proceeds to pay a deposit to the Company: 20% on Starter deals (£997), 15% on Launch deals (£2,497), 12% on Growth deals (£4,997), and 10% on Scale deals (£9,997+).
  2. Trigger: Commission becomes due only when the client makes their initial deposit payment to the Company. A booked call or enquiry alone does not trigger commission.
  3. Payment: Commission will be paid to the Representative within 7 days of the client's deposit clearing into the Company's account.
  4. Method: Payment will be made by bank transfer to the account details provided by the Representative.
  5. No cap: There is no upper limit on the commission a Representative may earn.
Example: A client referred by the Representative signs up for a £2,497 Launch website. The client pays a £1,249 deposit. The Representative earns £374 (15% of £2,497), paid within 7 days of the deposit clearing.

4. Attribution

  1. A lead is attributed to the Representative if they made the first introduction and the client has not previously been in contact with the Company.
  2. If there is a dispute over attribution, the Company's records (email timestamps, CRM logs, Google Sheet entries) will be used to determine the referring Representative.
  3. Leads must be logged in the Company's shared tracking sheet at the time of introduction to be eligible for commission.

5. Representative Obligations

The Representative agrees to:

  1. Represent the Company honestly and professionally at all times.
  2. Use only the approved call scripts, email templates, and materials provided by the Company.
  3. Not make any promises, guarantees, or commitments to prospects on behalf of the Company beyond what has been agreed in writing.
  4. Log all lead activity in the Company's shared tracking sheet promptly.
  5. Not disparage the Company, its services, or its clients.

6. Company Obligations

The Company agrees to:

  1. Provide the Representative with all necessary materials, scripts, and training to carry out their role effectively.
  2. Respond to booked calls and qualified leads promptly.
  3. Pay commission accurately and on time as set out in Section 3.
  4. Inform the Representative of the outcome of each lead within 7 days of a sales call taking place.

7. Confidentiality

The Representative agrees to keep confidential all information relating to the Company's clients, pricing, systems, and business operations, both during and after the term of this agreement. This includes not sharing client contact details with third parties.

8. No Exclusivity

This agreement is non-exclusive. The Company may appoint other sales representatives at any time. The Representative may work for other businesses provided this does not create a conflict of interest with the Company's services.

9. Termination

  1. Either party may terminate this agreement at any time by giving 7 days' written notice by email.
  2. Commission earned on leads introduced before the termination date will still be paid in full once those clients have paid their deposits.
  3. No commission will be paid on leads introduced after the termination date.

10. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of England and Wales. Any disputes will be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of England and Wales.

11. Entire Agreement

This document constitutes the entire agreement between the parties regarding the Representative's appointment. It supersedes any prior verbal or written discussions. Any amendments must be agreed in writing by both parties.

12. Acceptance

This agreement is accepted digitally via the CrftdWeb offer system. By clicking "Accept Offer" on the link provided, the Representative confirms they have read and understood this agreement and agrees to be bound by its terms. The Company's records (including the timestamp, email address, and unique offer token) constitute proof of acceptance.
