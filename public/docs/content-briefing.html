Now that the agreement is signed and deposit received — thank you! — we're ready to start building.

To kick things off we need the content below from you. The sooner we have it, the sooner we can start and hit the timeline we agreed. Please aim to send everything within 5 business days.

1. Brand & Identity

Logo
Required
Your logo in the best quality version you have.
SVG or PNG preferred. On a transparent background if possible. If you don't have this, let us know and we'll work with what you have.
Brand colours
If you have them
Your primary brand colour(s) as hex codes, or just tell us the colours and we'll match them.
e.g. "Our main colour is a dark navy, and our accent is gold." Or: #1B2A4A / #C9A84C
Fonts / typography preferences
Optional
Any font preferences, or examples of sites whose typography you like.
If you don't have preferences here, we'll choose something that fits your brand and get your sign-off before building.

2. Written Content (Copy)

You can provide copy in any format — a Word doc, Google Doc, notes app, even a voice note. We'll format and structure it for the site. If you'd like suggestions or edits, just let us know.

Headline & tagline
Required
The main statement at the top of your homepage. What do you do and who do you do it for?
e.g. "Premium cleaning services for busy London homes." — Keep it punchy. 1–2 lines max.
About your business
Required
A short description of who you are, what you do, and why clients should choose you.
1–3 paragraphs is enough. Focus on the client — what problem do you solve for them?
Services / offerings
Required
List of your services with a short description of each. Include any pricing if you display it publicly.
A bullet list is fine. e.g. "1. Deep clean — full house top to bottom, 3–4 hours. From £199."
Any additional page copy
If applicable
Copy for any other pages included in scope — FAQ, team, pricing, etc.
Only what was agreed in the proposal. Let us know if you're unsure what's included.

3. Images & Media

Best formats JPG or PNG for photos. SVG for logos and icons. WebP is also fine.
Quality The larger the better — we'll compress for the web. Don't send tiny or watermarked images.
Photos of your work / team / premises
Required
Real photos perform better than stock. Send any professional or high-quality phone photos you have.
Portfolio shots, team photos, your workspace, before/after shots — anything that shows what you do.
Hero / banner image
If you have one
A strong image for the top of the homepage. Landscape, high resolution.
If you don't have one, we'll source a suitable stock image — just let us know the vibe.

4. Business Information

Contact details to display publicly
Required
Phone number, email address, and/or address — whatever you want visible on the site.
If you have a business email separate from personal, use that.
Social media links
If applicable
Full URLs to your Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, TikTok, etc.
e.g. instagram.com/yourbusiness — only include ones you actively use.
Google Business / reviews
Optional
A link to your Google Business profile if you have one, and 2–3 testimonials you'd like to show.
Reviews from Google, Trustpilot, or anywhere else. Copy and paste the text with the reviewer's first name.

5. Technical Access

Domain name
Required
Your domain (e.g. yourbusiness.co.uk) and where it's registered (GoDaddy, Namecheap, 123-reg, etc.).
We'll need DNS access to point it to the new site. We'll walk you through this step — it's straightforward.
Existing website access
If applicable
If you have an existing site we need to pull content from or redirect traffic away from.
Only required if we agreed to migrate content. Send login details securely — don't include them in an email, we'll provide a secure way to share.
