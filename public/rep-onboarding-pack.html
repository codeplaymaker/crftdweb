Your Role

Understand exactly what you are and are not responsible for.

You are a Business Development Representative. Your job is one thing: find businesses that need a website and book them on a discovery call with us.

You do not close deals. You do not handle pricing negotiations. You do not make design decisions. You find the prospect, qualify them, and pass them over. We take it from there.

How to Qualify a Prospect

Before you book a discovery call, make sure the prospect passes these four checks:

Question to AskGood AnswerDisqualifying Answer
"Do you currently have a website?" No, or yes but it's outdated / not working "I just had one built last month"
"What's the website for — what should it do for your business?" Get more customers, look professional, rank on Google "I just need something quick and cheap"
"If we could show you a design you loved, would you be ready to move forward?" Yes / within the next few weeks "Maybe next year" / "I'm just browsing"
"Are you the person who makes this decision?" Yes / Yes, me and my partner "I'd have to check with my board" / "Let me ask my boss"
Rule of thumb: If they need a website, have budget intent, can make the decision, and are ready within weeks — book the call. If two or more of those are missing, move on.

What This Role Is Not

  • Not a salaried position
  • Not a guaranteed income without effort
  • Not a role where we chase you — you own your output
  • Not a role where showing up is enough — results matter

Section 05

Scripts & Objection Handling

Know what to say before you pick up the phone.

Cold Call Opener

Keep it under 15 seconds. Your only goal is to earn the next 30 seconds of their time.

Opener 1 — Direct:
"Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] from CrftdWeb. I help businesses like yours get more customers through their website. I'm not trying to sell you anything right now — I just had a quick question. Do you currently have a website for [their business]?"
Opener 2 — Observation:
"Hi [Name], I came across [their business] and noticed you don't seem to have a website — or the current one might not be doing you justice. We work with businesses in [their industry] to fix exactly that. Worth a 15-minute chat to see if we can help?"
Opener 3 — LinkedIn/DM:
"Hey [Name] — saw your business and thought the online presence doesn't match the quality of what you actually do. We build websites that bring in real enquiries. Would you be open to a quick chat?"

Common Objections

What They SayWhat You Say
"I already have a website" "That's great — is it bringing you the customers you want? A lot of our clients had a site before they came to us. The difference is ours are built to convert, not just exist."
"How much does it cost?" "It depends on what you need — we have a Starter package at £997 for a single landing page, all the way up to full multi-page sites. The discovery call is free and takes 15 minutes — we'll give you an honest answer on what makes sense for your business."
"I can get one cheaper on Wix/Squarespace" "You absolutely can. But there's a reason most of those sites don't rank on Google or convert visitors into customers. Our sites are hand-coded, SEO-optimised, and built around your specific business goals. It's the difference between a business card and a salesperson that works 24/7."
"I need to think about it" "Totally fair. The discovery call is just 15 minutes, no commitment — we'll show you what's possible and you can decide from there. How's [day] at [time]?"
"I'm too busy right now" "I hear that a lot — and honestly, that's exactly why having a website that works for you matters. It brings in leads while you're busy doing the work. Can we lock in 15 minutes next week?"
"Send me an email" "Happy to — what's the best address? I'll keep it short. Just so I send you the right thing, is it more that you don't have a site yet, or that the current one isn't doing enough?"
Golden rule: Never argue. Acknowledge what they said, then redirect to value. Your job is to get the meeting, not win the debate.

What Changes at Each Level

Rep
You book the call. We close it. You earn commission on every deal that lands.
Senior Rep
Priority access to warm inbound leads. You mentor new reps and earn a bonus on their first 3 deals.
Closer
You run the full sales cycle — from call to close. Higher commission, bigger deals, direct client contact.
How fast can you move up? There's no fixed timeline. Some reps reach Senior in 4–6 weeks. It's based entirely on results, not time served. If you're closing deals and showing consistency, you'll be invited to level up.
