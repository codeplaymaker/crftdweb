Section 01

What We Do

Know the product before you sell it.

CrftdWeb is a boutique web design agency. We build premium, hand-coded websites and web applications for businesses that understand the value of exceptional design.

We are not a template agency. We are not competing on price. Our work is positioned at the premium end of the market — purposeful, minimal, and built to convert.

The one-line pitch: "Most business websites look fine and do nothing. We fix that."

What We Sell

Package What It Is Price Range Timeline
Landing Page Single page, one goal, one CTA £1,200 – £2,400 1–2 weeks
Business Website 5–7 pages, full SEO, conversion-focused £3,200 – £6,400 3–5 weeks
Web Application Custom app with auth, dashboard, database £6,400+ 6–10 weeks

Who We Sell To

Small to medium businesses across any industry that:

  • Have no website, or have an outdated one that isn't converting
  • Are serious about growth and understand investment
  • Make decisions quickly — typically business owners, not procurement departments
  • Value quality over cheapness
Who to avoid: Anyone who leads with "how cheap can you do it" or wants a website for under £500. They are not our client.
Section 02

Your Role

Understand exactly what you are and are not responsible for.

You are a Business Development Representative. Your job is one thing: find businesses that need a website and book them on a discovery call with us.

You do not close deals. You do not handle pricing negotiations. You do not make design decisions. You find the prospect, qualify them, and pass them over. We take it from there.

The Process

1

Find

Identify a prospect via cold outreach

2

Qualify

Do they need a site? Do they have budget intent?

3

Book

Get them on a discovery call with CrftdWeb

4

Close

We handle the call and close the deal

5

Get Paid

Commission hits your portal within 14 days

What This Role Is Not

  • Not a salaried position
  • Not a guaranteed income without effort
  • Not a role where we chase you — you own your output
  • Not a role where showing up is enough — results matter

Section 06

Conduct

You represent this brand. Act accordingly.

Required

  • Represent CrftdWeb professionally in all outreach and conversations
  • Be honest about what you know — escalate pricing questions to us
  • Log all leads and activity in the portal in real time
  • Respond to messages from CrftdWeb within 24 hours
  • Personalise your outreach — no copy-paste spam

Not Permitted

  • Making pricing commitments without confirmation from us
  • Misrepresenting our services, timeline, or capabilities
  • Mass-spamming prospect lists
  • Engaging prospects you cannot qualify
  • Sharing internal materials, pricing documents, or portal access
Zero tolerance: Reps who misrepresent the company or conduct themselves unprofessionally are removed immediately with no commission on outstanding deals.

Section 08

Getting Paid

Simple, transparent, trackable.

  • Commission is calculated on the client's confirmed project value at point of signed agreement
  • Payment is made within 14 days of client deposit received
  • Payment via bank transfer — details collected at confirmation
  • All commissions visible in your rep portal with status: Pending or Paid
  • Disputes or questions resolved within 48 hours of raising

Commission is Not Paid On

  • Prospects who attend a call but do not sign
  • Leads who request a quote but go cold
  • Deals where commission arrangement was not agreed before the call
  • Leads submitted without prior qualification
