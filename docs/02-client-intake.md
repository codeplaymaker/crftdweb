## The Problem

- **Why are they coming to you?** (in their words)
- **What's actually broken?** (your diagnosis after looking at their current site/situation)
- **What have they tried before?** (DIY, another agency, template, nothing)
- **What's the cost of doing nothing?** (losing leads, looking unprofessional, can't raise prices)

## Their Customer

- **Who buys from them?** (be specific — not "everyone")
- **What does their customer fear?** (wasting money, looking stupid, missing out)
- **What does their customer want?** (status, security, convenience, results)
- **Where does their customer hang out online?** (Google, Instagram, LinkedIn, referrals)

## The Goal

- **One action:** What's the single most important thing a visitor should do on this site? (book a call, sign up, buy, download)
- **Success metric:** How will the client know this worked? (leads per month, conversion rate, inquiries)
- **Timeline:** When do they need this live?

## Proof They Have

- **Testimonials/reviews:** (yes/no — how many, from where)
- **Case studies or results:** (numbers, transformations, before/after)
- **Logos or brand assets:** (existing logo, brand colors, fonts — or need new)
- **Photos/videos:** (real photos of team/product, or need stock/generated)
- **Awards, press, certifications:** (anything that builds trust)

## Competitive Landscape

- **Top 3 competitors:** (list their URLs)
- **What competitors do well:**
- **What competitors do badly:**
- **How is this client different?** (the real differentiator, not "we care more")

## Scope

- **Pages needed:** (list them)
- **Special functionality:** (forms, auth, dashboard, e-commerce, integrations)
- **Auth required?** (Firebase Auth — signup/signin/reset)
- **Database required?** (Firestore or Supabase)
- **Email integration?** (Resend API for contact forms, transactional emails)
- **AI features?** (OpenAI integration — adds to scope and cost)
- **Content:** (client provides copy, or we write it, or we guide and they write)
- **Ongoing needs:** (maintenance, updates, content changes after launch)

## Pre-Build Checklist

Before writing any code, confirm:

- [ ] I can articulate their customer's problem in one sentence
- [ ] I know the one action the site needs to drive
- [ ] I have (or have a plan to get) real proof/testimonials
- [ ] Scope is agreed and written down
- [ ] Payment terms are agreed
- [ ] Timeline is realistic
