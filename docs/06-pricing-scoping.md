## Scoping Questions (Ask Before Quoting)

1. **How many pages?** (determines tier)
2. **Do they need auth/database?** (Tier 2 vs Tier 3)
3. **Do they have copy?** (add copywriting or not)
4. **Do they have a brand?** (add brand identity or not)
5. **Do they have images/photos?** (need to source or not)
6. **What's their timeline?** (rush = premium)
7. **Do they need ongoing support?** (add maintenance retainer)
8. **What's their budget?** (qualify before investing time)

## Quoting Rules

1. **Never quote before the intake is filled out.** If they won't answer questions, they won't pay on time.
2. **50% upfront, 50% on launch.** No exceptions for new clients.
3. **Scope in writing.** If it's not in the scope doc, it's not included.
4. **Revisions are counted.** 1–3 rounds is fair. "Unlimited revisions" destroys profit.

## Quoting Rules

1. **Never quote before the intake is filled out.** If they won't answer questions, they won't pay on time.
2. **50% upfront, 50% on launch.** No exceptions for new clients.
3. **Scope in writing.** If it's not in the scope doc, it's not included.
4. **Revisions are counted.** "Unlimited revisions" destroys profit. 1-3 rounds is fair.
5. **Rush fee = 25-50% premium.** If they need it in half the time, charge accordingly.
6. **Say no to bad fits.** If the budget is $500 for a full site, refer them to a template.
7. **Site audit as a gateway.** Offer a paid audit first. If they like it, they hire for the rebuild.

## Red Flags (Walk Away)

- "Can you just do it for exposure?"
- "My nephew could do this but he's busy"
- "We need it tomorrow"
- "We'll pay when we start making money"
- "Can you match this Fiverr quote?"
- No clear business model or revenue
- Can't articulate who their customer is
- Want to "approve every pixel" (micromanagement)

## Green Flags (Great Client)

- Knows their customer well
- Has existing revenue or funding
- Respects your process
- Has real testimonials or proof to share
- Understands websites are investments, not expenses
- Responds to communications promptly
- Budget aligned with scope
